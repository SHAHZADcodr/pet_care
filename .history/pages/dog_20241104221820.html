Golden Retriever

Breed: Sporting

Tips: Regular exercise is essential; they love to play and swim.

Labrador Retriever

Breed: Sporting

Tips: Very friendly; needs a lot of socialization and exercise.

Beagle

Breed: Hound

Tips: They have a strong sense of smell and love to explore; training is important.

Bulldog

Breed: Non-Sporting

Tips: Low energy; enjoy short walks and have a calm temperament.
