Parrot

Species: Psittacidae

Tips:

  • Requires regular social interaction.

Canary

Species: Serinus canaria

Tips:

  • Enjoys a calm environment.
  • Requires a diet rich in seeds and greens.

Sparrow

Species: Passeridae

Tips:

  • Social birds that thrive in flocks.
  • Provide a varied diet for optimal health.

Cockatiel

Species: Nymphicus hollandicus

Tips:

  • Friendly and easy to train.
